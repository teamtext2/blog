What is Text2?
Updated on August 6, 2025

Text2 is a software company focused on researching and developing smart applications powered by large language models (LLMs).

As part of building a full ecosystem, Text2 specializes in:

  • Context-aware translation tools that adapt to real-world conversations and scenarios.
  • Conversational AI software capable of personalized, memory-driven interactions.
  • Browser-based and local-first AI tools that work offline, without relying on external servers.
  • Enterprise solutions for building custom chatbots, managing internal content, and automating communications.

Beyond technology, Text2 also develops integrated social utilities, PWA installable apps, and unified user interfaces across platforms.

We believe AI is more than a tool — it’s a companion for creativity, productivity, and knowledge exploration. Our mission is to make powerful, open, and accessible AI software available for everyone.

— The Text2 Team
